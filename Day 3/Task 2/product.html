SOFA

A sofa is a large, comfortable seating piece designed for multiple people, commonly found in living rooms, lounges, and offices. Typically upholstered in fabric, leather, or synthetic materials, sofas provide both style and functionality. They often feature plush cushions for added comfort, supported by a sturdy wooden or metal frame. Some sofas come with reclining options, built-in storage, or pull-out beds for extra versatility. The design of a sofa can vary from classic to contemporary, with options like sectional sofas, loveseats, or chesterfields catering to different preferences. Colors, patterns, and textures further enhance their aesthetic appeal, making them a focal point in many spaces.
